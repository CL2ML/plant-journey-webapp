The original idea for PlantJourney comes from Christoph and Sarah. They both love plants, but found themselves over and over in the position to have killed another beautiful innocent house plant. Fortunately, they know how to code and found a great team to develop the idea together. The prototype was developed during the project phase of the Digital Shaper Program by TechLabs.
