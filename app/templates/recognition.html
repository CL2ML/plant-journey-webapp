Snake plant

Sansevieria

The Snake Plant originally comes from Africa. You should make sure it's standing at a place where it gets only medium light. Best is to water the plant once a month, because it does not need much water. The Snake Plant cannot carry blossoms. It's recommended to re-pot the plant yearly in spring.

Careful:

It can be toxic hor humans, but not for pets.
